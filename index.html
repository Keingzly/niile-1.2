What we’re Building

Niile was conceived out of a popular pain point amongst young Nigerian adults looking to find real estate properties to buy or rent and the difficulties and complicated process in our current system. This has pushed us to create a better platform that serves Africans and fosters a healthier, more transparent real estate industry.

With Niile, acquiring verified property listings across Africa becomes as simple as booking a hotel room. Our platform combines data, technology, and human oversight to ensure transactions are seamless, secure, and stress-free, whether you’re buying, renting, or investing from within Africa or abroad.

Our roadmap is ambitious: starting in Nigeria, we aim to reshape real estate across the continent with a platform designed for trust, simplicity, and intelligent property management.
